Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you cater to?
We offer programs for all age groups - from K-12 students to university students and working professionals. Our programs are tailored to meet the specific needs of each group.
How long are the internship programs?
Our internship programs typically range from 3 to 6 months, depending on the role and project requirements. We also offer part-time options for students.
Do you provide certificates upon completion?
Yes, all participants who successfully complete our programs receive industry-recognized certificates that can boost their career prospects.
What is the fee structure?
Our fee structure varies by program. We offer competitive pricing and flexible payment plans. We also have scholarship opportunities for deserving candidates.
Can institutions customize the curriculum?
Absolutely! We work closely with institutions to customize our programs according to their specific requirements and learning objectives.
